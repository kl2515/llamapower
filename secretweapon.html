LLAMA POWER What else can they do?

Omen

Other than being a best friend or guardian, llamas can do extraordinary things less known to man. The above llama pictured (how he might look like) is known as The Great Llama Wizard. Rumor has it that The Great Llama Wizard was able to take down threats with one kick of the leg. It has been seen flying through the mountains and healing sick llamas and humans. The last time someone claimed to have seen The Great Llama Wizard was just about 200 years ago. But alas, even if The Great Llama Wizard is no more, people have noticed an increase in willpower in their llamas throughout the years. It is speculated that The Great Llama Wizard instilled courage into other llamas and told them to be brave in preparing for a fight that is greater than we've ever known. Maybe it'll be a good idea to befriend a llama soon...
