LLAMA POWER Find out about the power of the llama

As a Friend

Raised correctly, we are very friendly creatures and must say, pleasant to be around. You can be yourself around us.

Guard Llama

A single male llama is all you need to protect your herd of sheep! He'll keep an eye out for wolves and the like. Your sheep can sleep soundly.

Pack Llama

We don't mind carrying some weight for you while traveling. However, too much weight and we'll sit on the ground.
